Watermelon Feta Salad

As you can see on the picture, this beautiful Watermelon Feta Salad doesn't only look delicious but gives you all your calories you need in the day.

The recipe is quite easy and will guide you to make this beautiful piece of food.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Steps:

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and salt together in a large mixing bowl. Add tomatoes, arugula, and red onion; toss to coat. Gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
